About Us

Golden market Multi-Purpose cooperative society duly registered under the Ministry of Commerce and Industries to operate as a Cooperative with Members of like minds and it’s a subsidiary of First Profit Trading Consult Limited has been in operation since January 2020, registered with the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC).

Our Cooperative Society ensures that its members’ financial interest is well protected and efficiently managed by investing in profitable sectors like, Financial Market services, Real Estates services, Industrial Productions services, etc.

Golden market Multi-Purpose cooperative society is a responsible, sustainable and viable cooperative whose aim is to uplift the social and economic well-being of its members through effective and efficient delivery of products, financial services and programs. As a Cooperative Society, our striving motive is to provide goods and services that may be needed by our members in due time. We pride in assisting our members reach their full potentials for savings at any designated period. Join Golden market Multi-Purpose cooperative society with a minimum capital of N100, 000 and receive 15-20% interest of your contribution monthly within a duration of three (3) or (6) Months.

The Cooperative Society involves herself in profitable businesses and investments, which in turn generates interests to serve her members, ensuring they become globally competitive individuals in the field of financial economic progress.
